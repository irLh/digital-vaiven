“soy de mente clara.”

if my great grandmother on my grandfather’s side is made of azucar
then the mother of my grandmother is freshly ground pepper

at ninety three she makes money by selling treats in her little apartment
she dyes her hair just like grandma: red like her spirit

she is tough, takes very little shit
but her love shows in the way she stayed looking at me in our whole visit.

“te contrasté” my own grandmother said on the phone.


great-grandma Christina, 93, in Guaynabo.
